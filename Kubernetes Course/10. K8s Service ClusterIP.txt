K8s ClusterIP : A K8s service canhelp us group the pods together and provide a single interface to access the 
                pods in a group.

Service defin file.

apiVersion: v1
kind: Service
metadata:
  name: back-end
spec:
  type: ClusterIP
  ports:
    - targetPort: 80
      port: 80
  selector:
    app: myapp
    type: back-end


To create the service:
>kubectl create -f <service-defin-file-name>.yml

To list the services:
>kubectl get services

Now the service can be accessed using the ClusterIP or by service name.
